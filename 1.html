AMSTERDAM

Amsterdam is the capital and most populous city of the Netherlands with a population of 872,680[7] within the city proper, 1,558,755 in the urban area[8] and 2,480,394 in the metropolitan area.[9] Found within the province of North Holland,[10][11] Amsterdam is colloquially referred to as the "Venice of the North", attributed by the large number of canals which form a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
