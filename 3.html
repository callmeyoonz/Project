TILBUTG

Tilburg (Dutch: [ˈtɪlbʏrx] (About this soundlisten)) is a city and municipality in the Netherlands, in the southern province of North Brabant. With a population of 217,595 (31 January 2019), it is the second-largest municipality in North Brabant and sixth-largest in the Netherlands.
